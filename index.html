A commons for an industry of ledgers.

Insurance runs on spreadsheets, legacy systems, and code that never quite escaped the actuary's desk. We think it deserves better tooling — open, well-documented, tested, and free to use.

Indemnipy is a small, growing group of engineers and actuaries working on a family of Python libraries under permissive licences. No vendors. No paywalls. Just the plumbing the industry has always needed.
